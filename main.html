First Skydiving Experience

Gettin ready >_<

Meeting my instructor for the first time! Super hype to get up on the plane and fall from 15,000 feet in the air!

Am I really doing this?

Standing at the edge of the plane preparing to jump had my heart racing like crazy. Pretty sure my life flashed before my eyes at this point.

Free Falling

This is the point of no return. I had to make a banana form with my body and hope I didn't start tumbling in circles as I fell back to the earth.

We Stablized!

Had my banana in perfect form! Feeling that we were not going to start tumbling away I spread my arms out like the majestic bird I am!

The Chill Stage

Parachute out. Slow fall back to the earth. This moment was very calm and peaceful as we turned in the air and watched all the toy cars and building operate on the ground. Mission Jump out of plane from 15,000. Success!
